What is Mines Game? Complete Guide 2026

Mines is a popular instant-win game by Spribe. Based on the classic Minesweeper concept, players reveal tiles to find gems while avoiding hidden mines.

Disclaimer: Mines Tracker is independent and NOT affiliated with Spribe.

How Mines Works

  1. Choose how many mines (1-24) you want on the 5x5 grid
  2. Place your bet
  3. Click tiles to reveal gems
  4. Each gem increases your multiplier
  5. Cash out anytime, or risk revealing more gems
  6. Hit a mine = lose your bet

Game Statistics

  • RTP: 97.0%
  • Grid Size: 5x5 (25 tiles)
  • Mine Range: 1-24 mines
  • Max Multiplier: Depends on mine count
